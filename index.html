The state of Oklahoma was devastated by tornadoes which touched down in a four mile swatch in Moore, just south of Oklahoma City. Great devastation and loss of life occurred and our prayers go out to the families of the victims.

The Church of God stands ready to assist. Within hours of the storm, churches in the immediate area opened their doors to disaster relief and Operation Compassion had sent 25 semi trucks of water, blankets, food and cleaning materials. The staging site for these trucks will be the Rolling Hills Church of God, where heavy equipment will also be staged until the go ahead is given to move closer to the site.

For those who wish to give in this effort, the Church of God has set up a way to give directly on this site. 100% of donations received will go to the disaster relief effort.
